  == Development Environment ==
  * KEILMDK Version 5.12 or above.
  * Programmer/Debugger: ST-LINK V2
  * Programming/Debugginginterface: JTAG/SWD
  
  == How to use ==
  1. Connect the USB TO UART Port to your PC using a USB cable. PL2303 driver should
     be installed to the PC. 
     * Baud rate: 115200
     * Data bits: 8
     * Stop bits: 1
     * Parity: None
     * Flow control: None
  2. Enter 10 characters to the serial from your PC then the development board will 
     respond and print them out via the serial port.
